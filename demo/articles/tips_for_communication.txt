<ul>
<li> Ask questions!</li>
<li> Be clear and specific about your respective needs.</li>
<li> Be open and honest about your feelings.</li>
<li> Recognize that each individual sees things from a different point of view.</li>
<li> Accept and respect the difference in opinions.</li>
<li> Maintain eye contact when meeting in person.</li>
<li> Listen and learn.</li>
<li> Allow time to talk with your mentee without interruptions.</li>
<li> Show an interest in what he or she has to say.</li>
<li> Listen for feeling and tone in speech, not just the words.</li>
</ul>
